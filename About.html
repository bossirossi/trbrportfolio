About Rossi

Hello and thank you for being here

I am Denver based, and studied Applied arts with a major in Ceramics in the historic, art-rich charming city of Troyan, Bulgaria. Currently completing Graphic Design studies, I loves using vibrant colors in distinct and unexpected combinations to create a style that is unique to her. I employ mostly acrylics, but I likes to venture into a number of materials such as oil, oil pen, graphite, micron pen, colored pencils, ink, and watercolors; integrating styles to achieve a distinctive and memorable impression on the viewer. I founded Rodazz.le, my jewlery brand, and am exploring mix media, photography and metal manipulation as well. My work is best known for centering and fascination with the human body in every aspect. My exploration of the face and its future could be found in most of my artwork.
